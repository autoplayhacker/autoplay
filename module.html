Features

Charger Module is a regulator board that is frequently used in do-it-yourself charger models. This board, which is very reliable and easy to use, has a lithium battery charge protection feature and intelligent over-temperature protection features. At the same time, this board has over-voltage, over-current, overload and short-circuit protections. While the connected battery is charging, the red warning LED on it flashes regularly, and when fully charged, the red warning LED lights up continuously.
